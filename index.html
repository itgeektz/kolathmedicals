Welcome to Kolath Medicals
Your Reliable Medical Shop with Strong Commitment and Experience in Bringing Quality Medicines
Kolath Medicals is an ISO 2015 certified medical shop located in Kadavanthra, Kochi. At Kolath, we have a controlled temperature storage facility to keep the products and medicines safely so that their quality is not compromised. This is to comply with the industry regulations and pharmaceutical safety guidelines. Having been in business for over 17 years, we pride ourselves on our holistic approach to health and providing the best possible medical care to the customers.

We are well stocked with medicines, manned by competent staff with computerized systems. We provide home delivery facilities for the required customers. Also, all items are offered at 15% to 40% discount over M.R.P and Insulin medicines are offered at 18% to 23% discount.
